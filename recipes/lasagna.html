Lasagna Recipe

Description

This meat lasagna is a cheesy and meaty meal! Perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

It is easy to make and satisfies everyone.

Ingerdients

  • Beef
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Seasoning
  • Spagetti sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef until browned, then add marinara sauce and let it simmer.
  3. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, beef sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  5. Let it cool for a few minutes, then serve and enjoy!
